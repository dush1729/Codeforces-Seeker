# Privacy Policy for CF Seeker

**Last updated:** March 3, 2026

## Overview

CF Seeker is a mobile application that allows users to track Codeforces competitive programming users and contests. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

CF Seeker collects and stores the following data **locally on your device**:

- Codeforces usernames you choose to track
- Contest and user data fetched from the public Codeforces API
- App preferences and settings

## Third-Party Services

CF Seeker uses the following third-party services:

- **Codeforces API** — to fetch publicly available user and contest data. See [Codeforces terms](https://codeforces.com/).
- **Firebase Analytics** — to collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., app opens, screen views). No personally identifiable information is collected.
- **Firebase Crashlytics** — to collect anonymous crash reports to help improve app stability.

## Data Storage

All user-entered data (tracked usernames, preferences) is stored locally on your device. No personal data is transmitted to our servers.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

## Children's Privacy

CF Seeker does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at [https://github.com/dush1729/Codeforces-Seeker/issues](https://github.com/dush1729/Codeforces-Seeker/issues).
